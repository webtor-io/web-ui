Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Webtor ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following data depending on how you use Webtor:

  • Email address — collected when you sign in with Google or Patreon, used solely for authentication.
  • Personal library — content you save to your account, including its metadata (titles, file lists). You control what is added and removed.
  • Watch and rating history — when you mark movies or series as watched or rate them, we store a reference to the film identifier (e.g. IMDB id), your rating signal, and the watch timestamp.
  • AI recommendation queries — when you use the AI Discover feature, we process the text you type to generate personalized suggestions and to enforce daily usage limits.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Email — to authenticate your account and communicate about your subscription.
  • Library — to store and organize the content you choose to save.
  • Watch history — to power the "continue watching" feature, to display your watched/rated state across the interface, and to ground AI recommendations in your actual taste.
  • AI queries — to generate film suggestions via our AI provider (see section 3) and to manage your daily request quota.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties, except as required by law.

When you use the AI Discover feature, your recent watch history (film titles, release years, and rating signals — up to 40 entries) along with your query text are sent to Anthropic, our AI provider, to generate personalized recommendations. No email address, user identifier, IP address, or any other personally identifiable information is included in these requests. Anthropic processes this data under their API data usage policy, which prohibits training on API inputs.

AI Discover is entirely opt-in — no data is sent to Anthropic until you actively tap a suggestion chip or type a query on the Discover page.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely and protected against unauthorized access. Watch history and AI usage data are associated with your account and are not publicly accessible.

5. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data (including watch history and AI usage data) at any time from your profile page.

6. Analytics

We use Umami, a privacy-focused, self-hosted analytics tool, to understand how visitors use Webtor. Umami does not use cookies, does not collect personal data, and all data is aggregated — no individual user can be identified.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
