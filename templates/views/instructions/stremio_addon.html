How to connect Stremio Addon

Want to add more content to your Stremio experience? Follow these simple steps to connect external Stremio addons to expand your streaming library:

  1. Go to https://stremio-addons.com/
  2. Browse and select the addon you want to install
  3. Copy the addon's manifest URL with "Copy link" button
  4. Paste the URL in the "Stremio Addons" section at your profile page and click "Add Addon"

Once added, the addon content will be available through your Stremio integration, expanding your streaming options with additional sources and catalogs.

Note: This feature is available only for paid users.
Note: Gathering streaming URLs can take some time (up to 10 seconds). Be patient.
