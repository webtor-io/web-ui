Vault

Vault is a storage system in Webtor that allows you to:

  • save torrents for a long time,
  • watch content without waiting for seeders,
  • support needed torrents with points,
  • have guaranteed access to files.

Vault Points (VP)

  • 1 Vault Point = 1 GB
  • Points are provided as part of your subscription or other activities (the amount depends on your subscription tier)

How saving to Vault works

  1. Click the "Keep This Torrent Available" button next to the torrent you want to save.
  2. Confirm the number of points required (for example, if the torrent is 10 GB, you need to deposit 10 VP).
  3. The funded points are frozen for {{ .FreezePeriod | duration }}. The freeze is automatically lifted when the torrent is successfully transferred to the Vault.
  4. Webtor transfers the torrent to the Vault (if the transfer fails within {{ .TransferTimeoutPeriod | duration }}, VP is returned).

Claiming Vault Points back

  • Points can be claimed back if they are not frozen.
  • If after claiming points back the amount is less than required to keep the torrent, it will be marked as expired and deleted after {{ .ExpirePeriod | duration }}.
  • When a torrent is deleted, all pledges associated with it are also deleted.

What if my subscription expires

  • Points that were invested in torrents lose their power and the torrent is marked as expired and deleted after {{ .ExpirePeriod | duration }}.
  • If the subscription is renewed, the points become active again and the torrents funded by them are no longer expired.
