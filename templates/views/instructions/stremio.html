How to connect Webtor to Stremio

You can stream your personal torrent library from Webtor directly in Stremio. Here's how to do it:

  1. Copy your personal addon link from your Webtor profile.
  2. Open Stremio and go to the Add-ons section.
  3. Click the Add Addon button in the top-right corner.
  4. Paste the addon link and press Enter. Done!

Once installed, your library will appear in Stremio under the Webtor.io addon – ready to stream.

⚠️ Note: This feature is available only for paid users.
⏳ Streaming via Webtor may differ from regular Stremio addons. It can take some time to warm up the torrent before playback starts.
