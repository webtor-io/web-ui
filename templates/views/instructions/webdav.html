How to connect Webtor via WebDAV

Access your torrent library just like a regular folder. With WebDAV, you can browse, stream, and manage your files directly from your favorite apps.

  1. Copy your personal WebDAV URL from your Webtor profile.
  2. Install a WebDAV client on your device (see recommendations below).
  3. Connect using your WebDAV URL and start browsing your library.
