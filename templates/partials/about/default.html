How to download torrents with Webtor.io

With Webtor.io it is easy to convert your torrent-file or magnet-url into direct download link in 3 simple steps without need to use torrent-client:

  1. Locate the torrent file on your computer
  2. Upload it to Webtor.io
  3. Click on the file link to download

What is Webtor.io?

Webtor.io is a browser-based torrent tool that helps you download, stream, and access torrent content without installing a torrent client. It works with both .torrent files and magnet links, and can generate direct download links (DDL) for simple HTTP downloads.

This makes Webtor useful on mobile devices, restricted environments, and whenever you want a quick download link or instant playback for video files.
