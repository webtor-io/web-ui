Explained

What is an online torrent player?

An online torrent player is a web-based video player that can open and play video files from torrents directly in your browser. Unlike desktop media players such as VLC or MPC, there is nothing to install — the player runs entirely in the browser.

Webtor.io transcodes video on the server in real time using FFmpeg, converting formats like MKV, AVI, MP4, WEBM, MOV, and WMV into browser-compatible HLS streams. This means virtually any video format plays smoothly without codec packs or plugins.

Safety

Is the online torrent player safe to use?

The Webtor torrent player runs in your browser and never exposes your device to the torrent swarm. All torrent connections are handled server-side, and video is delivered to you over encrypted HTTPS. No VPN is required, and no data is stored on your device beyond the browser cache.
