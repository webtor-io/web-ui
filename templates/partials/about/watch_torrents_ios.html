How to watch torrents on iPhone or iPad with Webtor.io

iOS does not allow traditional torrent clients, but with Webtor.io you can stream torrent videos directly in your browser in 3 simple steps:

  1. Open Webtor.io in Safari on your iPhone or iPad
  2. Paste a magnet link or upload a torrent file
  3. Start watching the video instantly

Why torrents are hard to use on iOS

iOS restricts background processes, file access, and peer-to-peer networking, which makes classic torrent clients impractical or unavailable on iPhones and iPads. Downloading large files is also inconvenient due to storage and system limitations.

As a result, many torrent apps for iOS are limited, unreliable, or require complex workarounds.

How Webtor streams torrents on iOS

Instead of downloading torrent data directly on your device, Webtor.io handles torrent connections and buffering on the server side. Video files are streamed over standard HTTP, which works smoothly in Safari on iOS.

This approach allows instant playback without installing apps, managing files, or waiting for a full download to complete.

Why streaming torrents on iOS is better than downloading

Streaming torrent videos on iOS is often faster and more convenient than downloading files. It avoids storage limits and lets you start watching immediately.

  • no torrent apps required
  • no large files stored on your device
  • works directly in Safari
  • instant playback for movies and TV shows

Is it safe to watch torrents on iOS?

When you stream torrents on iOS with Webtor.io, your iPhone or iPad does not connect directly to the torrent swarm. Torrent data is processed on the server side and delivered as a video stream.

Watch torrents on iOS without apps

Because Webtor works entirely in the browser, you can watch torrent videos on iOS without installing any apps from the App Store. All you need is Safari and a magnet link or torrent file.
