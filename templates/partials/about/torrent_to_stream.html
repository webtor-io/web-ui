Explained

What does "torrent to stream" mean?

Torrent to stream is the process of converting torrent video content into an HLS (HTTP Live Streaming) or DASH video stream on the fly. Instead of downloading the entire file, the video is segmented and delivered in small chunks that play sequentially in any compatible player.

Webtor.io performs this conversion in the cloud using FFmpeg-based real-time transcoding. The result is an HLS stream delivered in small segments, enabling instant playback without waiting for the full file to download.

Benefits

Why convert torrents to streams?

Raw torrent files are not streamable — they require a BitTorrent client and full download. Converting to HLS unlocks instant playback, device compatibility, and integration possibilities.

Real-time HLS transcoding for instant playback
Works with Stremio and other HLS players
Developer API for embedding streams
Real-time conversion — no pre-processing
Safety

Is torrent to stream conversion safe?

With Webtor.io, all torrent-to-stream conversion happens on the server. Your device never connects to the torrent swarm, and the resulting HLS stream is delivered over encrypted HTTPS. No VPN is needed, no ports to configure, and no peer-to-peer traffic reaches your network.
