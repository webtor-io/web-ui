Explained

What does "stream torrent online" mean?

Streaming a torrent online means watching video content from a torrent without downloading the entire file to your device first. The torrent data is fetched by a cloud server and converted into an HLS video stream that plays natively in any modern web browser.

Webtor.io handles all the heavy lifting — BitTorrent connections, real-time transcoding with FFmpeg, and HLS segment delivery — so you get smooth playback regardless of the original video format (MKV, AVI, MP4, WEBM, and more).

Safety

Is it safe to stream torrents online?

When you stream torrents online with Webtor.io, your device never connects to the torrent swarm directly. All data is fetched by Webtor servers and delivered to your browser over encrypted HTTPS. No VPN is needed, no ports to open, and no peer-to-peer traffic touches your network.
